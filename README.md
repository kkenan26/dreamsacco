# DREAMSACCO
DreamSacco is a modern and didgital solution that is designed to transform traditional saving groups(SACCOs) into a transparent, disciplined and efficicent experience for the youth in Uganda.

# THE PROBLEM
Many young people struggle to maintain consistent saving habits due to manual and messy record keeping in notebooks, lack of transparency regarding funds and the complexity of management of loans and calculation of interests.

# THE SOLUTION
The DreamSacco is menat to digitalize the traditional saving group experience by providing;

*Automated Contributions: Streamlined payment tracking and automated reminders.

*Credit Worthiness Engine: A smart algorithm that ranks members based on their contributuon history, repayment records and activity to determine loan eligibility.

*Full transparency:  A clear dashboard allowing every member to track group balances and individual status of contribution in real-time.

*Financial Guidance: Built in tools for setting goals, calculating 'what-if'scenarios and learning basic money skills.

# PROJECT FEATURES

*Authentication and Security: Multi-factor authentication and secure OTP verification.

*Group Management: Tools for creating private or public groups, inviting members and managing administration roles.

*Smart Loan System:  Automated loan request flows and repayment schedules ranked by credit score.

*Goal Tracking: Visual progress bars ad milestone notifications to keep users motivated.

The SACCO management application project is designed using flutter and firebase.
